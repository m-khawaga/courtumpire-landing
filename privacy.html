Privacy at a glance

No account is required. CourtUmpire does not use ads. In the current early testing version, CourtUmpire does not intentionally use analytics, cookies, external tracking, cloud sync, payments, or backend services.

Match information

The app allows you to enter match information such as player names, scores, match log entries, and match settings. This information is stored locally on your device. CourtUmpire does not receive, transmit, or access this match information.

Local match data remains on your device until you delete the match data, clear the app data, or uninstall the app.

PDF reports

PDF reports are generated on your device. You choose whether to save, export, or share them.

If you share a PDF report outside CourtUmpire, that sharing is controlled by you and by the sharing app or service you select.

Website access data

When you visit courtumpire.com, the website hosting provider may process technical access data such as your IP address, browser information, the date and time of access, and server logs. This is used to deliver the website securely and reliably. CourtUmpire does not use website analytics, advertising cookies, or tracking cookies.

Email contact

If you contact CourtUmpire by email, your email address and message content are processed only to respond to your inquiry.

Your choices

  • You can avoid entering real player names.
  • You can delete local app data from your device.
  • You choose whether to export or share PDF reports.

Your privacy rights

Where applicable, you may request access to your personal data, correction or deletion of it, restriction of its processing, or object to its processing. You may also lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority. For private-sector matters in Bavaria, the likely supervisory authority is the Bavarian State Office for Data Protection Supervision (BayLDA).

No automated decision-making

CourtUmpire does not use automated decision-making or profiling.

Children

CourtUmpire is not specifically directed at children. If the app is used by minors in training, club, or tournament contexts, adults should avoid entering unnecessary personal information.

Future features

This policy must be updated before CourtUmpire adds analytics, cloud sync, crash reporting, ads, payments, accounts, contact forms, or third-party SDKs.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when the app changes. The latest version will always be available on this page.
